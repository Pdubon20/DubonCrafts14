Services

Installation

Flawless from prep to plank.

  • Solid & engineered wood installation
  • Layout planning (straight, diagonal, herringbone optional)
  • Acclimation & moisture testing
  • Subfloor leveling & prep
  • Nail, glue, or floating systems as appropriate

Staining & Sanding

Dust-controlled, color-true, durable.

  • HEPA dust capture sanding
  • Custom color matching to samples
  • Water- or oil-based systems (matte → gloss)
  • Low-odor & VOC-free options
  • Protective multi-coat finish

Hand-Scraped Finish

Authentic, time-worn character—by hand.

  • Subtle undulation with eased edges
  • Hand-tooled texture that hides wear
  • Pairs well with white oak, red oak, and hickory
  • Optional wire-brush or bevel accents
  • Sealed in your choice of sheen
